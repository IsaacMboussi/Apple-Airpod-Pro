Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect AirPods Pro to my device?

Simply open the charging case near your iPhone, iPad, or Mac, and tap "Connect" on the screen. Your AirPods Pro will automatically connect to all your Apple devices signed in to your iCloud account.

Can I use AirPods Pro with non-Apple devices?

Yes, AirPods Pro can be used with any Bluetooth-enabled device. However, some features like automatic switching and spatial audio are only available with Apple devices.

How do I switch between noise cancellation modes?

Press and hold the force sensor on either AirPod to switch between Active Noise Cancellation and Transparency mode. You can also control this through your device's Control Center.

What's included in the box?

AirPods Pro, Charging Case, Three pairs of silicone ear tips (small, medium, large), Lightning to USB-C Cable, and Documentation.
